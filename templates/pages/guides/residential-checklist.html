Home Cleaning Tips

  • ✓ Declutter First: Remove items before cleaning for easier access and faster work.
  • ✓ Work Top to Bottom: Start from ceiling and work downward to avoid re-cleaning.
  • ✓ Use Timers: Set time limits to stay focused and motivated.
  • ✓ Keep Supplies Handy: Have all cleaning products ready before starting.
  • ✓ Use Right Products: Different surfaces need different cleaners for best results.
