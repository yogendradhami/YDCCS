Post-Construction Cleaning Tips

  • ✓ Hire Professionals: Post-construction cleaning is intensive. Professional cleaners have the right equipment and experience.
  • ✓ Timing: Schedule cleaning after all construction is complete, but before final inspections and handover.
  • ✓ Safety: Ensure the property is safe before cleaning begins (no exposed hazards, loose debris, etc.).
  • ✓ Quality Products: Use commercial-grade cleaning products designed for post-construction cleaning.
  • ✓ Documentation: Take photos/videos of the completed clean for your records.
