Professional Tips for Streak-Free Windows

  • ✓ Timing: Clean on a cloudy day or when sun isn't directly on windows. Direct sun causes solution to dry too quickly, causing streaks.
  • ✓ Newspaper vs Cloth: Crumpled newspaper is excellent for streak-free drying. Coffee filters also work well.
  • ✓ Water Temperature: Warm water works better than cold for dissolving soap and dirt.
  • ✓ Squeegee Technique: The key is keeping the blade clean. Wipe the blade after every pass.
  • ✓ Order Matters: Clean outside first, then inside. This prevents dirty water from dripping on clean areas.
