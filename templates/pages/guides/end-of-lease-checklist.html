Bond Recovery Tips

  • ✓ Professional Cleaning: Consider hiring professionals for best results. The cost is often less than losing bond money.

  • ✓ Check Your Lease: Review your lease agreement for specific cleaning requirements.
  • ✓ Act Quickly: Clean as soon as possible after move-out for best results.
  • ✓ Use Professional Products: Commercial-grade cleaners work better than household products.
