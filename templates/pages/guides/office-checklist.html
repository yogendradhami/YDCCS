Professional Cleaning Tips

  • ✓ Declutter First: Remove personal items and clutter before cleaning to access all surfaces.
  • ✓ Work Top to Bottom: Start from high surfaces (ceilings, shelves) and work down to floors.
  • ✓ Use Right Cleaning Agents: Different surfaces require different products. Always follow manufacturer guidelines.
  • ✓ Disinfect High-Touch Areas: Pay extra attention to door handles, light switches, and shared equipment.
  • ✓ Maintain Equipment: Clean and maintain your cleaning tools regularly for best results.
  • ✓ Schedule Consistently: Regular cleaning prevents buildup and maintains a professional appearance.
